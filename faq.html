General Questions

Bazaar Odisha is a digital platform designed to connect the people of Odisha with local service providers, skilled workers, and products from Self-Help Groups (SHGs). Our mission is to empower the local economy and make it easier to find trusted services and authentic goods.

Yes, browsing for services, products, and workers on Bazaar Odisha is completely free for users. Businesses and SHGs can also create a basic listing for free.

Our platform is hyper-focused on Odisha. We are not just a marketplace; we are a community-building tool designed to strengthen the local economy by promoting local talent and entrepreneurship, especially women-led Self-Help Groups.

For Buyers & Hirers

Each business and worker profile has a "Contact Now" or "Call Now" button. Clicking this will allow you to call them directly using their listed phone number.

You can add products to your cart from the SHG Marketplace and proceed to checkout. We handle the payment securely, and the SHG is notified to prepare your order for delivery.

Bazaar Odisha is a discovery platform that connects you with providers. Your service agreement is directly with the provider. However, we encourage you to leave an honest review on their profile to help future customers. For any serious issues, please contact our support team.

For Sellers & Service Providers

Click the "Add Your Business" button in the header. You'll be guided through a simple form to create your profile. Our team will review it, and once approved, it will be live on the platform.

You will need basic information about your business, such as your name, contact number, location, and a description of your services. Adding clear photos of your work or products is highly recommended to attract more customers.

For SHG products sold through the marketplace, we process customer payments and transfer the funds directly to the SHG's registered bank account after deducting a small service fee. For direct services, you handle the payment directly with the customer.
