Bazaar Odisha

Empowering Odisha's Local Economy

Bazaar Odisha is not just an app — it is a digital revolution tailored to Odisha’s grassroots economy[1]. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing a simple, accessible, and powerful platform for local service providers, Self-Help Groups (SHGs), and micro-businesses to connect with consumers across the state[1].

What We Do

Thousands of skilled workers, small shopkeepers, and women-led SHGs are the backbone of Odisha's economy, yet they often remain invisible in the digital world[1]. We provide them with the tools to create a digital presence for free, helping them reach more customers and grow their businesses sustainably[1].

Our Key Features

  • Find Local Services: Easily find and contact skilled professionals like plumbers, electricians, and tailors in your area[1].
  • Bazaar Cart: Discover products from local stores and SHGs, and connect with sellers directly via WhatsApp to make a purchase[1].
  • SHG Hub: A dedicated section to showcase and promote the incredible handmade, organic, and eco-friendly products made by women's Self-Help Groups[1].
  • Community Forum: A place for users to ask questions, share recommendations, and support each other, building a stronger local community[1].

Our Vision

We envision a digitally inclusive Odisha where every entrepreneur, no matter how small, has an equal opportunity to thrive. By fostering a self-reliant local ecosystem, we aim to boost the rural economy, promote micro-entrepreneurship, and empower communities to build a better future with dignity and visibility[1].

Join us in supporting local. Together, we can make a difference.
