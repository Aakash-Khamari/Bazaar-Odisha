Privacy Policy for Bazaar Odisha

Last Updated: August 2, 2025

Welcome to Bazaar Odisha. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information You Provide: When you register a business, list a service, post in the community forum, or contact us, you may provide us with personal information such as your name, phone number, location, and business details.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you access and use the platform, such as your search queries, pages viewed, and interactions with other users or listings.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect the content you create on the platform, including community posts, replies, and reviews.
  • Local Storage Data: Features like your Wishlist and Settings are saved locally on your device using browser `localStorage` to enhance your experience. We do not transmit this data to our servers in the current version.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the Bazaar Odisha platform.
  • To connect you with service providers, SHGs, and other users.
  • To display business listings and community content as intended.
  • To personalize your experience, such as showing you relevant local services.
  • To improve our platform and develop new features.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your privacy is our priority. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

  • Publicly Visible Information: Information you choose to make public, such as your business name, phone number in a listing, or your name on a community post, will be visible to other users of the platform.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with your explicit consent, such as when you choose to contact a service provider via WhatsApp or a phone call.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We use administrative and technical measures to safeguard your personal information. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can review and update your business listing information at any time.
  • You can control your app preferences, such as language and notifications, in the Settings page.
  • You can clear your browser's `localStorage` to remove data like your wishlist, though this may impact your user experience.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the "Contact" option in the app menu.
